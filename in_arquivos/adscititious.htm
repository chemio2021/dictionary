Adscititious (ad-si-TISH-uhs) adjective
 
Derived from outside; external; additional.
 
[From Latin adscitus, past participle of adsciscere (to admit or adopt),
from ad- (toward) + sciscere (to seek to know), from scire (to know).
Ultimately from the Indo-European root skei- (to cut or split) that also
gave us schism, ski, and shin.]
