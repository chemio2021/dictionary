Aggrate

PRONUNCIATION:
(uh-GRAYT) 
 
MEANING:
verb tr.: To please or gratify.
 
ETYMOLOGY:
From Italian aggradare (to please), from Latin aggratare, from gratus (pleasing, grateful). Earliest documented use: 1590.
