Alazon

PRONUNCIATION:
(AL-uh-zon) 
 
MEANING:
noun: A person characterized by arrogance, braggadocio, lack of self awareness, etc.
 
ETYMOLOGY:
After Alazon, a stock character in ancient Greek comedy. Earliest documented use: 1911.
