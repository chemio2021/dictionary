Anserine

PRONUNCIATION:
(AN-suh-ryn, -rin)
MEANING:
adjective:
1. Of or relating to a goose.
2. Stupid; silly.
 
ETYMOLOGY:
From Latin anser (goose). Earliest documented use: 1839.
