Artless

PRONUNCIATION:
(ART-les) 
 
MEANING:
adjective:
1. Without guile; sincere; simple.
2. Free of artificiality.
3. Lacking art or skill.
 
ETYMOLOGY:
From art, from Latin ars (art) + less, from Old English leas (without). Earliest documented use: 1586.
