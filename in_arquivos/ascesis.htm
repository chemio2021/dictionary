Ascesis

PRONUNCIATION:
(uh-SEES-is) 
 
MEANING:
noun: The practice of severe self-discipline or self-control. Also spelled as askesis.
 
ETYMOLOGY:
From Greek askesis (exercise or training), from askein (to exercise or work). Earliest documented use: 1873.
