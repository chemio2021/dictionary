Asinine

PRONUNCIATION:
(ASS-uh-nyn)
MEANING:
adjective: Extremely stupid; ridiculous.
 
ETYMOLOGY:
From Latin asinus (ass), from the general reputation of donkeys -- entirely undeserved -- as being stubborn and stupid.
 
USAGE:
"With banks crashing and unemployment soaring, Prohibition felt like what it was -- an asinine waste of time and money."
Dan Gardner; You Can't Tell Us Drug Legalization is Impossible; The Ottawa Citizen (Canada); Apr 10, 2009.
