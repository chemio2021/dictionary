Audiophile

PRONUNCIATION:
(AW-dee-oh-fyl) 
 
MEANING:
adjective: One who has a keen interest in high-fidelity sound reproduction.
 
ETYMOLOGY:
From Latin audio- (sound) + -phile (love, lover of). Earliest documented use: 1951.
