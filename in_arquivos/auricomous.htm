Auricomous

PRONUNCIATION:
(au-RI-kuh-muhs) 
 
MEANING:
adjective: Relating to golden hair.
 
ETYMOLOGY:
From Latin auricomus, from aurum (gold) + coma (hair). Earliest documented use: 1864.
