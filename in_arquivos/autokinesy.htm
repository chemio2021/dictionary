Autokinesy

PRONUNCIATION:
(au-toh-KIN-uh-see) 
 
MEANING:
noun: Self-propelled or self-directed motion or energy.
 
ETYMOLOGY:
From Greek auto- (self) + kinein (to move). Earliest documented use: 1678.
