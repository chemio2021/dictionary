Avast (uh-VAST) interjection

   Stop (used as a command to stop or desist).

[From Dutch hou vast (hold fast), from houd vast.]

-Guest Wordsmith John (Ol' Chumbucket) Baur (chumbucketATtalklikeapirate.com)
