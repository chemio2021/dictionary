Bandersnatch

PRONUNCIATION:
(BAN-duhr-snach) 
 
MEANING:
noun: 1. A fast and ferocious wild creature. 2. An uncouth or bizarre person.
 
ETYMOLOGY:
Coined by Lewis Carroll in his 1871 novel Through the Looking-Glass. The name of a fictional creature.
