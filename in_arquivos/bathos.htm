Bathos

PRONUNCIATION:
(BAY-thas, -thos) 
 
MEANING:
noun: An abrupt descent from lofty or sublime to the commonplace; anticlimax.
 
ETYMOLOGY:
From Greek bathos (depth). Earliest documented use: 1638.
