Blue streak (bloo streek) noun
 
1. Something moving very fast.
 
2. A rapid and seemingly endless stream of words.
 
[Or unknown origin, perhaps an allusion to a bolt of lightning.]
 
-Anu Garg (words at wordsmith.org)
 
"The amiable young teen talks a blue streak to the camera or anyone who
happens to be nearby, his eyes darting about and his expression morphing
from moment to moment."
Limited Movie Runs: Bill the Kid; Seattle Post-Intelligencer; Jan 10, 2008.
