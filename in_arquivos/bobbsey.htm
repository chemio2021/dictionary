Bobbsey twins (BOB-zee twins)

 

   Two people who appear, think, or do alike.

 

[From the characters in a children's book series created in 1904 and

published under the pen name of Laura Lee Hope. Here's an excellent website

about the Bobbsey twins: http://pw1.netcom.com/~drmike99/aboutbobbsey.html ]

 

  "Jarrin' John and Battlin' Bill were the Bobbsey Twins of campaign

   reform, one from the left, one from the right, joined in symbolic

   handshake at Claremont, N.H., pledging their troth to smite the evil

   of soft money in political campaigns."

   David Nyhan, What Happened to Bradley?, The Boston Globe, Mar 3, 2000.
