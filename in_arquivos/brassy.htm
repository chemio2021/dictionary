Brassy (BRAS-ee) adjective

 

   1. Made of or resembling brass.

   2. Resembling the sound of brass instruments.

   3. Brazen; bold; impudent.

   4. Showy; pretentious.

 

[From brass, from Middle English bras, from Old English bræs.]
