Tarry (TAR-ee) verb intr.

 

   To delay, stay, or wait.

 

verb tr.

 

   To wait for.

 

noun

 

   A short stay; a sojourn.

 

[From Middle English tarien/taryen (to delay).]

 

tarry (TAR-ee) adjective

 

   Of, like, or smeared with tar.

 

[From tar + -y.]
