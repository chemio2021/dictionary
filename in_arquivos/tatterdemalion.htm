Tatterdemalion

(tat-uhr-di-MAYL-yuhn, -MALEE-uhn) adjective

   Ragged, tattered.

noun

   A person in ragged clothes.

[From Middle English tater, from Old Norse toturr (rag).
The origin of demalion is uncertain.]
