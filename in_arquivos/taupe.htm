Taupe (toap, rhymes with rope) noun
 
   A brownish gray, similar to the color of moleskin.
 
[From French taupe (mole), from Latin talpa.]
 
Today's word in Visual Thesaurus: http://visualthesaurus.com/?w1=taupe
