Terra firma (TER-uh FUR-muh) noun

 

   Solid ground; dry land.

 

[From Latin terra (earth) + firma, feminine of firmus (solid).

Ultimately from Indo-European root ters- (to dry) that is the

source of words such as territory, terrace, turmeric, and toast.]
