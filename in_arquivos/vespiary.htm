Vespiary (VES-pee-er-ee) noun

   A nest or colony of wasps.

[Blend of Latin vespa (wasp) and apiary (a place where bees are kept).]

Italian scooter company Piaggio named their first product Vespa because
they felt it looked and perhaps sounded like a wasp.

-Anu Garg (garg wordsmith.org)

  "[William Bullitt] had moved into Charles Bedaux's Château de Candé,
   a perfect vespiary of Nazi spies and collaborators."
   Charles Higham; The Duchess of Windsor: The Secret Life; John Wiley
   & Sons; 2004.
