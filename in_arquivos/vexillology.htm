Vexillology (vek-si-LOL-uh-jee) noun

 

   The study of flags.

 

[From Latin vexillum (flag), diminutive of velum (covering) + -logy]

 

Can you identify three words that are related to today's word in the

following sentence?

 

            "The bride removed the voile veil to reveal her lovely face."

 

The words are voile, veil, and reveal, all of which are descendents of Latin

velum and involve the idea of covering (or uncovering in case of "reveal").

-Anu
