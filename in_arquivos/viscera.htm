Viscera

PRONUNCIATION:
(VIS-uhr-uh)
 
MEANING:
noun:
1. The internal organs located in the main cavities of the body, especially those in the abdominal cavity.
2. The interior parts.
 
ETYMOLOGY:
Plural of Latin viscus (flesh, internal organ). Earliest documented use: 1651.
