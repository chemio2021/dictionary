Willy-nilly (WIL-ee NIL-ee) adverb, adjective
 
1. Whether willing or not.
 
2. Haphazardly.
 
[From contraction of "will ye/he/I nill ye/he/I", from will (to be willing)
and nill (to be unwilling).]
