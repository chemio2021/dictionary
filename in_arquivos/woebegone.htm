Woebegone (WO-bi-gon) adjective
 
   Affected with, or exhibiting, woe.
 
[From Middle English woe + begon (beset).]

Woe-Be-Gone! Sounds like the perfect name for a drive-thru pain-relief
outlet. What a shame, the word means the opposite of what it appears to be.
 
There is a variety of Australian carpet shark called wobbegong (named from
an Australian Aboriginal language). If one ever comes in touch with this 
fish, a woebegone status is certain.
