Wonk (wongk) noun

 

   An expert who studies a subject or issue thoroughly and excessively.

 

[Of unknown origin.]

 

This word is most often encountered in the term "policy wonk". There

are many speculations about the origin of the word, for example an

acronym for WithOut Normal Knowledge, or the reverse spelling of the

word know, but these claims are not supported by evidence.
