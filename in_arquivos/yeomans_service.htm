Yeoman's service

PRONUNCIATION:
(YO-muhnz SUHR-vis)

 

MEANING:
noun: Efficient, useful, or loyal service.

 
ETYMOLOGY:
From yeoman (servant or assistant), probably a blend of young + man, or from ga (region) + man.

 
USAGE:
"Commissioner Ralph Carpenter, who sits on the reform commission, is a good man and hard worker. He has done yeoman's service for the governor."
Toni Gold; A Better Bureaucracy Won't Fix The DOT; Hartford Courant (Connecticut); Nov 11, 2007.
